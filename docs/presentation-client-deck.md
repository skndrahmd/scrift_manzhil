### The Security Flow

1. **Resident creates pass** via WhatsApp — takes 30 seconds
2. **Resident forwards the pass** directly to their visitor
3. **Visitor shows the pass** at the gate on their phone
4. **Guard verifies** the pass ID — instant confirmation
5. **Resident gets notified** when their visitor arrives
6. **Complete visitor log** maintained automatically

**No more unauthorized entry. No more paper logbooks. No more "who approved this visitor?" questions.**
